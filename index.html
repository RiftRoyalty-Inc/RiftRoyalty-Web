Rift Royalty

The First League Companion
With Gamers, By Gamers

RiftRoyalty is a community platform for League of Legends players. Offers news, statistics and tools to improve the gaming experience.
Users can connect, share strategies, and follow other players' progress.
With premium features like advanced analytics and customization, RiftRoyalty takes gaming to the next level.
What We Are Building

Rift Royalty Features

News and Interesting Info

In our application you will have on your main screen real-time news information from the LoL universe, the highest ranked players and the best champions in each role of the moment.

Champions Info

You will have access to the list of champions and their details such as the most recent builds, as well as the runes and priority to level up skills, as well as information on the matchups in which the champion is strong or weak.

Items Info

You can see the current game items in a list where you can filter by class and see their statistics and description

Profile Stats

You will have detailed information about your profile, friends you play with the most, most played characters or the statistics of your last games.

Easy Navigation

The application is designed so that it is easy to navigate through it using a bottomTab that allows you to move to all the screens
